Who We Are

RDG Business Holding is a diversified Ethiopian business group operating across construction, import, and the coffee industry. Founded with a vision to deliver quality and reliability, we've grown into a trusted partner for businesses nationwide.

Our commitment to excellence drives every division, from landscape construction to coffee lab equipment supply and our upcoming specialty coffee roasting brand.
