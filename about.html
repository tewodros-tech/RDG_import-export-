About RDG Business Holding

Building Ethiopia’s future through construction, import & premium coffee innovation

RDG Business Holding is a diversified business group operating in construction, import, and the coffee industry. The company currently manages two major businesses: RDG Construction, a general contractor focused on landscape construction and outdoor development works, and RDG Import, specializing in electrical equipment, coffee laboratory tools, and commercial roasting machinery.


As part of its expansion strategy, RDG Business Holding is establishing a third division focused on coffee roasting, branding, and supplying premium Ethiopian coffee to local and international markets with world-class quality standards.

Founder & CEO

Robi is the visionary founder of RDG Business Holding. He leads the company with a strong commitment to innovation, quality, and long-term development.

Under his leadership, RDG has grown into a multi-sector business group focused on building sustainable infrastructure, improving import solutions, and developing Ethiopia’s coffee industry for global markets.
