About RDG Business Holding

Building Ethiopia's future through construction, import, and coffee excellence

RDG Business Holding is a diversified Ethiopian business group operating in construction, import, and the coffee industry. The company currently manages two major businesses: RDG Construction, a general contractor mainly focused on landscape construction and outdoor development works, and RDG Import, which specializes in importing electrical equipment, coffee laboratory equipment, and commercial coffee roasting machinery.

As part of its expansion strategy, RDG Business Holding is establishing a third division focused on coffee roasting, branding, and supplying high-quality Ethiopian coffee products to local and international markets.

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of professional landscaping services in Ethiopia, renowned for innovative designs, exceptional craftsmanship, and commitment to environmental sustainability.

Our Mission

To transform the Ethiopian landscaping industry by offering comprehensive, personalized, and sustainable solutions that exceed expectations and create lasting landscapes.

Key Projects & Clients

RDG Construction has successfully delivered major projects including UNITY PARK - Parking Lot and Mixed Use, ELEPHANT PAW LODGE (Chebera Churchura), East Africa Bottling Share Company recreational space development, and National Cement Lemi Project landscape concept development.
