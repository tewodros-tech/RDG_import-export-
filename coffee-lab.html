Our Coffee Journey

RDG Coffee is born from a passion to share Ethiopia's rich coffee heritage with the world. We are establishing a state-of-the-art coffee roasting, branding, and export division that celebrates the authentic flavors of Ethiopian specialty coffee.

From sourcing premium beans directly from highland farmers to implementing modern roasting techniques, RDG Coffee will deliver unparalleled quality in every cup. Our commitment to sustainability, fair trade, and excellence drives every step of our process.

With advanced coffee laboratory equipment and professional cupping facilities, we ensure that every batch meets international standards while preserving the unique character of Ethiopian coffee.
