RDG Construction

RDG Construction is a premier general contractor specializing in landscape architecture, residential construction, and premium finishing works. With over a decade of experience, we transform visions into reality, delivering exceptional quality and craftsmanship for every project across Ethiopia.

Landscape Design

Custom landscape designs that blend natural beauty with functional outdoor living spaces, tailored to each client's unique vision and property requirements.

Landscape Plantation

Professional selection and installation of native and exotic plants, trees, and greenery for vibrant, sustainable environments that thrive year-round.

Expert installation of patios, walkways, retaining walls, and decorative stone features that enhance property value and curb appeal.

Residential Construction

Complete construction services for residential buildings from G+1 to G+5, ensuring structural integrity, modern design, and timely delivery.

Finishing Works

Premium interior and exterior finishing including professional painting, ceramic tile installation, and decorative treatments for elegant spaces.

Project Management

End-to-end project oversight from initial concept through completion, ensuring timelines, budgets, and quality standards are consistently met.
