About RDG Business Holding

RDG Business Holding is a diversified Ethiopian business group operating in construction, import, and the coffee industry. The company currently manages two major businesses: RDG Construction, a general contractor focused on landscape construction, and RDG Import, specializing in importing electrical equipment, coffee laboratory equipment, and commercial coffee roasting machinery.

As part of its expansion strategy, RDG Business Holding is establishing a third division focused on coffee roasting, branding, and supplying high-quality Ethiopian coffee products to local and international markets.
