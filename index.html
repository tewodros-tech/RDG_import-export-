About RDG Business Holding

Engineering excellence, global import solutions & authentic Ethiopian coffee heritage

RDG Business Holding is a diversified business group operating in construction, import, and the coffee industry. The company currently manages two major businesses: RDG Construction, a general contractor mainly focused on landscape construction and outdoor development works, and RDG Import, which specializes in importing electrical equipment, coffee laboratory equipment, and commercial coffee roasting machinery.


As part of its expansion strategy, RDG Business Holding is establishing a third division focused on coffee roasting, branding, and supplying high-quality Ethiopian coffee products to local and international markets. With a commitment to innovation and premium standards, the group drives impact across multiple industries.

Robel Demissie

Founder & CEO

Robel Demissie is the visionary founder and CEO of RDG Business Holding. With an unwavering passion for infrastructure development, industrial innovation, and Ethiopia’s coffee legacy, he leads the group with a future-focused mindset.

Under his leadership, RDG has evolved into a multi-sector powerhouse — bridging modern construction, global imports, and premium coffee craftsmanship. Robel's strategic direction ensures that every division operates with integrity, quality, and a deep commitment to local and international partners.

Building Ethiopia's tomorrow, one project, one product, and one relationship at a time.
